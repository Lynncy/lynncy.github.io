From Dec 2025 to Jan 2026, my colleagues and I delivered a series of four tutorials about AI video generation for the faculty of Meilong Experimental School Affiliated to ECUST (华东理工大学附属闵行梅陇实验学校). These tutorials primarily focused on China’s leading generative AI tools such as Alibaba’s Qwen, ByteDance’s Jimeng (Dreamina), Suno, and MiniMax’s Hailuo AI.
